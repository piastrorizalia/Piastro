About Me

Hello! My name is Rizalia Mae B. Piastro, 20 years old, born on November 14, 2005. I live in Main Barrio, San Agustin, San Jose, Occidental Mindoro, and currently study at OMSC San Jose Campus as an IT student. Before diving into IT, I explored the world of Architecture, which deepened my love for arts and creativity — a passion I now express through web development.

I am blessed with a loving family who inspires me every day. My mother, Nerissa B. Piastro, is a dedicated teacher at Naibuan Elementary School, and my father is a hardworking farmer. We share our home with three dogs — Yanie, Barnie, and Combie — and more than 15 cats, who bring so much joy and energy to our household.

I enjoy a variety of hobbies that help me grow both personally and creatively. I love watching sunsets by the sea, joining fun runs, and riding my motorbike — especially joy rides that give me a sense of freedom. I also love cooking and experimenting with new dishes to improve my skills, and exploring coffee shops and local food spots. My favorite foods include spaghetti, shawarma, pizza, cake, fries, and many more.

I enjoy arts and creativity in all forms, playing chess and traditional games like sungka. My favorite colors, maroon and pink, reflect my bold, vibrant, and energetic personality. I take pride in blending creativity with technology in my web projects, using every opportunity to challenge myself and learn something new.

I believe that life is about growth, curiosity, and embracing new experiences. Whether through technology, arts, or personal hobbies, I strive to create, learn, and share my journey with others.
